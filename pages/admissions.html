How to Apply

4 Simple Steps to Join EduCore

1
Register Online

Fill in the online registration form on this page. A registration confirmation will be sent to your email.

2
Document Submission

Submit required documents — previous marksheets, birth certificate, transfer certificate, and passport photos.

3
Assessment & Interview

A friendly aptitude assessment (Class III+) and a brief interaction with the principal for Classes VI and above.

4
Offer Letter & Fee Payment

Receive your offer letter and complete fee payment to confirm your seat. Welcome to the EduCore family!

Eligibility

Age Criteria

Nursery: Minimum 3 years as of April 1, 2026
LKG: Minimum 4 years as of April 1, 2026
UKG: Minimum 5 years as of April 1, 2026
Class I: Minimum 6 years as of April 1, 2026
Class II–V: Passed the preceding class from a recognised school
Class VI–VIII: Merit + aptitude test + previous marksheets required
Class IX: Minimum 60% in Class VIII. Interview required.
Class XI: Minimum 75% aggregate in Class X CBSE boards
