About EduCore

Our Story

A Legacy Built on Purpose

Founded in 1994 by Dr. Vikram Anand, EduCore School of Excellence began with a simple but powerful vision: to create an institution where every child could discover their potential and be equipped to make a positive difference in the world.

From our humble beginnings with 80 students and 6 teachers in a single building, we have grown into one of Delhi's most respected CBSE-affiliated schools, now serving over 1,200 students from Nursery through Class XII across a sprawling 10-acre campus.

Our success is not measured only in board results — though those are consistently outstanding — but in the leaders, innovators, artists, and compassionate human beings who carry the EduCore spirit into the world.
